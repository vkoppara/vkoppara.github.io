International Day Celebration

Celebrating unity in diversity at the Annual International Day 2025.

Schedule

9:00 AM

Opening Ceremony & Flag Display

Grand opening ceremony featuring flag displays from around the world, welcome speeches, and the lighting of the unity torch. Students will parade with flags representing their cultural heritage.

Duration: 60 minutes Location: Main Assembly Hall
10:00 AM

Cultural Performances & Traditional Dances

Spectacular showcase of traditional dances, music, and cultural performances from different countries. Students will perform folk dances, sing traditional songs, and display cultural costumes.

Duration: 90 minutes Location: School Auditorium
11:30 AM

International Food Festival

Taste authentic cuisines from around the world prepared by students and families. Experience diverse flavors, learn about food traditions, and enjoy cultural delicacies from various countries.

Duration: 90 minutes Location: School Cafeteria & Courtyard
1:00 PM

Language Exchange Activities

Interactive language learning sessions where students teach and learn different languages. Participate in language games, practice basic phrases, and discover the beauty of multilingual communication.

Duration: 90 minutes Location: Classrooms & Language Lab
2:30 PM

Traditional Games & Sports

Experience traditional games and sports from different cultures. Play kabaddi, cricket, football, traditional board games, and learn about sports heritage from various countries.

Duration: 90 minutes Location: Sports Ground & Activity Areas
4:00 PM

Closing Ceremony & Awards

Culminating celebration with award presentations, recognition of outstanding participation, group photographs, and a unity pledge. Closing remarks and vote of thanks to all participants.

Duration: 60 minutes Location: Main Assembly Hall
