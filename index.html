INTERNATIONAL DAY

Why Should we Celebrate

Why is it celebrated

International Day promotes global awareness, cultural understanding, and celebrates the diversity that makes our world community stronger and more united.

Importance of diversity

Diversity enriches our perspectives, promotes tolerance, and helps students develop global citizenship skills essential for the modern world.

History of International Day

Established to foster international cooperation and understanding, this day highlights the importance of cultural exchange and global harmony.

How it is celebrated

Through cultural performances, traditional food exhibitions, flag displays, and interactive activities that showcase different countries and cultures.

Cultural exchange

Students learn about different traditions, languages, and customs, building bridges of understanding and respect across cultures.
